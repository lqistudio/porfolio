👾 About Me

I'm Sebastián, though in the creative world I go by LQI. I’ve been creating visual content for over 6 years, blending design, motion graphics, video editing, and branding to tell stories with identity. My approach merges the technical with the emotional. I don’t just execute — I observe, interpret, and translate ideas into visual pieces that resonate. I work from strategy, attention to detail, and creative instinct.
